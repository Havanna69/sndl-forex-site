Our story

When it all started

SNDL Forex & Travel is emerging as one of the trusted foreign exchange and travel service providers in India, serving both individual travellers and business clients with reliability and professionalism. As a Reserve Bank of India (RBI) authorized Full Fledged Money Changer (FFMC), we are empowered to provide foreign exchange in compliance with the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA) guidelines. We are committed to delivering secure, transparent, and fully regulated transactions, ensuring complete peace of mind for our customers. Our mission is to make every journey—whether for business, leisure, or international travel—smooth, secure, and financially convenient through exceptional forex and travel solutions.
